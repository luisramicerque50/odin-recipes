Prisma Recipe

The most nibbler cat of the house

This main course is perfect as a bodyguard.

Ingredients

  • Petite
  • Bites
  • Bitter
  • Pretty

Recipe Instructions

  1. Grind bitterness and add a cup of prettiness to it
  2. Add a dash of petite and a pinch of bites
  3. Bake for 20 minutes and serve cold
